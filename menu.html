About

Daawat Restaurant is one of those few restaurants that serve non-vegetarian delicacies in the city of Buddha. Daawat dishes out a range of fine Tibetan, Chinese, Thai and Indian cuisine. Do not forget to try their tofu, stuffed paratha, sattu ki roti and fresh fruit juices which are extremely popular among locals and travellers alike. So anyone with an appetite for non-vegetarian dishes must visit this restaurant when in Gaya.
